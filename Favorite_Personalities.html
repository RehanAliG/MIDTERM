My Favorite Personalities



Quaid-i-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah


Our top hero is the father of the nation. His name is Quaid I Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah. Pakistan came into existence because of his efforts. He was born on December 25, 1876.



Dr.Allama Muhammad Iqbal


Our next hero, in the heroes of Pakistan list, is a visionary. He is known for his famous address at Allahabad. Yes, he is Dr. Muhammad Iqbal.



Fatima Jinnah (Māder-e Millat)


In this list of heroes of Pakistan, Fatima Jinnah comes next. She was a strong advocate of the Muslim homeland. The great lady stood by her brother Muhammad Ali Jinnah in good times and bad times. she promoted the Two-Nation Theory.



Liaquat Ali Khan


The next hero in our list of renowned heroes of Pakistan is Liaquat Ali Khan. He was also the first Prime Minister of the country. He gave his contribution as a statesman, a political theorist, and a lawyer.



Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan


Our next hero in the list of heroes of Pakistan is known as the father of Pakistan’s Nuclear Bomb. Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan is a renowned Pakistani nuclear engineer. His efforts established a balance of power between India and Pakistan.



Abdul Sattar Edhi


If you aim to help the poor, follow the path of Abdul Sattar Edhi. He is one of the great heroes of Pakistan, the founder of the Edhi Foundation. For a long time, this organization has been serving humanity. This organization provides rehabilitation centers, orphanages, and shelters. The place is a safe space for all those in need.
